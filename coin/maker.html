Maker is an Ethereum-based protocol that issues the stablecoin DAI and is governed by the MakerDAO community, which consists of holders of MKR tokens. The MakerDAO community's decision to temporarily increase annual yields from 3.19% to 8% on August 6 is largely responsible for this increase.
