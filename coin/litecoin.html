Litecoin halving’s impact on the LTC price doesn’t have the same impact as Bitcoin’s halving has on the BTC price. Litecoin halving trails by 3 years from Bitcoin halving. The upcoming event is the 3rd Litecoin halving event, while Bitcoin’s 4th halving is to happen in March/April 2024. Both Litecoin and Bitcoin operate on different levels of scarcity. While Litecoin’s supply is fixed at 84 million, Bitcoin’s total supply is capped at 21 million, making it more scarce than Litecoin. As the supply of LTC is more, miners may get higher volumes to access compared to that of BTC.
