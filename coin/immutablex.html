Immutable X(IMX)

Description:

Immutable X is a layer 2 scaling solution designed to make NFT minting and GameFi dApps deployment seamless on the Ethereum blockchain. The layer 2 solution uses Stark zk roll-ups. IMX is an ERC-20 utility token built for the purposes of rewarding pro-network activities on Immutable X, such as trading, liquidity provision, and building applications. Unlike other scaling platforms Immutable X has an open order book that shares details of all details across the globe for enhanced liquidity on the protocol network. Immutable X is an Ethereum grade layer 2 that helps users mint NFTs at zero gas fee and user-friendly API. In addition to great tools, Immutable X allows users to create and distribute ERC – 20 and ERC – 721 on a large scale.
